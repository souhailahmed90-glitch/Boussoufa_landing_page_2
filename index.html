About Boussafa S.A.R.L

Founded in 2002, Boussafa S.A.R.L is a leading distributor of cosmetics, perfumes, skincare, haircare, and beauty accessories in Morocco.

Based in Derb Omar, the nation’s largest cosmetics wholesale hub, the company is recognized for more than 23 years of reliability, professionalism, and strong market expertise.

We connect global beauty brands with the fast-growing Moroccan market through trust, performance, and long-term partnerships.
